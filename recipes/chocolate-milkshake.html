Chocolate Milkshake

A 300x300 sized chocolate milkshake

Ingredients

  • 4 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup chocolate syrup
  • 1 drop peppermint extract

Directions

Step 1

Combine ice cream, chocolate syrup, and peppermint extract in a blender; blend until smooth

Step 2

Pour into 2 glasses and serve.
